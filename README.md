## 🎹 Setting Up Your Workspace

Open the application after the install finishes. You will see an icon on your desktop.

1. Double-click the Reason-Plus-2026 icon.
2. Sign in with your account credentials.
3. Open the "Preferences" menu from the top toolbar.
4. Select "Audio" from the menu.
5. In the "Audio Device" box, choose your sound card driver.
6. Adjust your buffer size. A lower number helps with recording instruments. A higher number helps if you run many tracks at once.
7. Click "Close" to save settings.

## 🎼 Creating Your First Project

The software opens with a blank rack. You build your song by connecting devices here.

1. Press F6 to view the main Rack.
2. Click the "Create" button at the top of the window.
3. Choose an instrument, such as a synthesizer or a drum machine.
4. Connect a keyboard or use your mouse to play notes.
5. Click the "Record" button in the transport bar to capture your ideas.
6. Use the "Sequencer" window to edit your timing and notes.

## 📂 Managing Your Files

Reason-Plus-2026 saves files in a format that keeps your audio and settings together. Always save your work often.

1. Click "File" in the top left corner.
2. Choose "Save" or "Save As."
3. Pick a folder on your drive for your music projects.
4. Name your file and click "Save."

If you need to share your music, use the "Export Song" feature. This creates an MP3 or WAV file. These files play on any media player.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If the software does not work, check these common fixes.

- Audio issues: Ensure your headphones or speakers connect to the correct port. Check if the device is set as the default in Windows Sound Settings.
- Slow performance: Close other open applications. Web browsers can use a lot of memory.
- File errors: Ensure you have enough disk space. Delete old files if your hard drive is full.
- Installation fails: Restart your computer and try the installation process again.

Your settings should persist between sessions. Contact the support team via the official website if problems continue after you try these steps.
